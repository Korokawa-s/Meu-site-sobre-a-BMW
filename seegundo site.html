THE M LEGACY

Born on the Track

The BMW M story began in 1972 with the founding of BMW Motorsport GmbH. What started as a racing division soon translated its track expertise to the road, creating the most thrilling performance cars in BMW's lineup.

The first true M car, the M1 supercar, debuted in 1978 with a mid-engine layout and striking Giugiaro design. This was followed by the iconic E28 M5 in 1984, establishing the M formula of combining everyday usability with racetrack performance.

"M stands for Motorsport. But it also stands for Magic, for Mystique, for Maximum performance."

- BMW M Division

M PERFORMANCE TECHNOLOGY

POWER & TORQUE

M engines are hand-built by specialists, featuring high-revving characteristics and immediate throttle response. The current S58 engine produces up to 503 HP.

DRIVETRAIN

M xDrive intelligently combines rear-wheel drive dynamics with all-wheel drive traction. The Active M Differential ensures optimal power distribution.

LIGHTWEIGHT

Extensive use of carbon fiber reinforced plastic (CFRP) for roofs, hoods, and interior components reduces weight while increasing rigidity.

TRACK-READY BRAKING

M Compound brakes with 6-piston front calipers provide fade-resistant stopping power. Optional carbon-ceramic brakes offer even greater performance.

ADAPTIVE SUSPENSION

M-specific adaptive suspension with electronically controlled dampers adjusts to road conditions and driving style within milliseconds.
